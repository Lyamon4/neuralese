# Neuralese research

`Neuralese-ISEF-2026.pdf` is the 33-page project paper submitted by Mikhail Isakov and Rakhim Nurmukhanbetov under the supervision of Dina Amirkanova.

The paper describes:

- the accessibility-versus-control problem in AI education;
- Neuralese's visual graph, dataset editor, simulations, Axon mentor, lessons, and ONNX export;
- Section Reuse and Fused SuperGraph optimizations for concurrent classroom training;
- a pedagogical evaluation with 83 students;
- measured learning gains and systems performance.
