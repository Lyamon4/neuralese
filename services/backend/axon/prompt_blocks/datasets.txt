## Built-In Dataset Identifiers

Use exact lowercase names only. Do not alias, capitalize, or invent dataset names.

- `mnist`: 70,000 samples. Input: 28x28 grayscale image. Output: digit, 10 classes (`0`..`9`). Storage: numpy.
- `iris`: 150 samples. Input: 4 numeric features (`sepal_length`, `sepal_width`, `petal_length`, `petal_width`). Output: species, 3 classes (`setosa`, `versicolor`, `virginica`). Storage: numpy.
- `titanic`: 891 samples. Input: 8 normalized tabular features. Output: survived, 2 classes (`no`, `yes`). Storage: numpy.
- `car_track`: 5,400 samples. Input: 8 raycast sensor distances (`ray_0`..`ray_7`, 0..1 floats). Output: continuous controls (`steer`, `throttle`). Storage: numpy.
